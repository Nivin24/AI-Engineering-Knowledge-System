# Exercises

This directory contains practical exercises for **Chapter 00 – Welcome to AI Engineering**.

## Learning Objectives

These exercises reinforce the concepts introduced in the chapter and encourage readers to think like AI engineers.

## Suggested Exercises

### Exercise 1

Explain AI Engineering in your own words.

---

### Exercise 2

Compare the responsibilities of:

- Software Engineer
- Machine Learning Engineer
- AI Engineer

---

### Exercise 3

Draw the AI Engineering lifecycle and explain each stage.

---

### Exercise 4

Research three AI products and identify the technologies used to build them.

---

### Exercise 5

Create a personal AI Engineering learning roadmap based on your current skills and future goals.

## Notes

These exercises are intentionally open-ended.

The objective is to encourage reasoning, research, and practical thinking rather than memorization.
