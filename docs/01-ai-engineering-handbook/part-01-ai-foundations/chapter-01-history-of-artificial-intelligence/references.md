# Seminal Research Papers

The following papers have significantly influenced the history of Artificial Intelligence.

- Alan Turing — _Computing Machinery and Intelligence_ (1950)
- John McCarthy et al. — _A Proposal for the Dartmouth Summer Research Project on Artificial Intelligence_ (1955)
- Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams — _Learning Representations by Back-Propagating Errors_ (1986)
- Krizhevsky, Sutskever & Hinton — _ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks_ (2012)
- Vaswani et al. — _Attention Is All You Need_ (2017)
