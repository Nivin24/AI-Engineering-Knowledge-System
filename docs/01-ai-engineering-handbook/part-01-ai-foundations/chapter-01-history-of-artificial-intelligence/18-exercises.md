# 18 — Exercises

## Overview

This chapter introduced the historical evolution of Artificial Intelligence, from its philosophical foundations to today's AI agents and autonomous systems.

The exercises in this section are designed to reinforce understanding, encourage critical thinking, and help you apply historical insights to modern AI engineering.

Rather than focusing only on memorization, these activities emphasize reasoning, comparison, communication, and engineering decision-making.

---

# Exercise Set 1 — Knowledge Check

These questions help reinforce the core concepts covered in the chapter.

1. Define Artificial Intelligence in your own words.
2. Why is the Dartmouth Conference considered the birth of AI?

4. Explain the purpose of an Expert System.

6. How does Machine Learning differ from Symbolic AI?
7. Why did Deep Learning become practical after decades of limited progress?
8. What made the Transformer architecture revolutionary?
9. What is a Foundation Model?
10. How do AI Agents differ from Generative AI systems?
