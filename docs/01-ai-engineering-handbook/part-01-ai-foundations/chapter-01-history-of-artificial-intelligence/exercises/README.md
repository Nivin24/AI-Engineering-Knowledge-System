# Exercises

This directory accompanies **Chapter 01 — History of Artificial Intelligence**.

The primary exercise document is:

- `18-exercises.md`

Readers are encouraged to complete the exercises in order.

Recommended progression:

1. Knowledge review
2. Concept comparison
3. Timeline reconstruction
4. Engineering reflection
5. Architecture thinking
6. Research activities
7. Career reflection
8. Communication practice
9. Mini project
10. Challenge exercise

The exercises are designed to reinforce engineering thinking rather than memorization.

Solutions are intentionally not provided so that readers can develop independent reasoning and discussion skills.
